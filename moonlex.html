MOONLEX

A product story and visual identity system for a consumer hardware brand shaped to feel precise, soft, and quietly premium.

Overview

MOONLEX explores a calmer way to present consumer tech: clear hierarchy, measured spacing, and a tone that feels designed rather than advertised.

Category
Product story / Identity
Focus
Consumer hardware, launch communication, visual system
Role
Visual strategy, layout system, art direction
Year

Approach

The page uses a large image-led opening, then shifts into concise blocks of text so the project reads quickly without losing mood.

This keeps the experience aligned with the rest of the site: restrained, editorial, and easy to scan on both desktop and mobile.
